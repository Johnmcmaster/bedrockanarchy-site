Server expectations.

This is an anarchy server, but these notes explain moderation boundaries and account safety basics.

Policy

What applies

  • Anarchy gameplay is allowed: raiding, PvP, griefing, and base hunting.
  • No doxxing, real-world threats, or illegal content.
  • No account sharing with people you do not trust.
  • Bedrock only. Use bedrockanarchy.org on port 19132.
